01 // DATA_COLLECTION_PROTOCOL

When you use the transmission form (contact form), the system collects the information you explicitly provide: your designation (SENDER_ID / Name) and your return address (RETURN_ADDRESS / E-mail). This data is used solely to process your message and route a response back to you.

02 // PROCESSING_AND_SECURITY

All data submitted through the form is processed directly by our server-side scripts and delivered straight to the administrator's secure mailbox. We do not sell, trade, lease, or share your personal data with any third-party entities for marketing or analytical purposes.

03 // COOKIES_AND_LOCAL_STORAGE

This website is built with a strict focus on minimalism. We do not use persistent tracking cookies, marketing scripts, or third-party analytics (such as Google Analytics). The only local data utilized is the browser's temporary session storage (`sessionStorage`), which ensures the navigation menu state is maintained when returning from subpages.

You retain the absolute right to request access to your transmitted data, demand its rectification, or request its complete erasure from our communication logs. To execute this protocol, please send a direct transmission to the general inquiry e-mail address listed on our contact page.
